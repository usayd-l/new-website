About

XTCY Studios came to me with a very rough idea of what they wanted. As a company that was just starting up they lacked a identity and branding. This is what the first part of our project focused on.

After experimenting with various designs and colour palletes we created brand guidelines that would server as the benchmark for future designs.

Description

The first phase of our project consisted of designing the brands logo, social media page and all other graphics. This included Instagram and Facebook posts, Highlights and even marketing content.

After completeting the design part of the project we started working on the website. Our initial concept for the website was to keep it minimal but comprehensive at the same time, matching the philosophy and identity of the brand. The websit was very simple but Javascript and SASS heavy. Utilising these frameworks I was able to enhance the user experience and overall feel of the site.
